What is Flirting Singles?

Flirting Singles is an online dating mobile application and an online dating website.

  • Both the app and website were built based on what actual users said in interviews that they wanted or didn't want when it comes to online dating.
  • Our goal is to provide users a unique and interactive dating experience by offering many different ways for singles to meet, interact, and connect on a more advanced level.
  • Our top priorities are to provide users with the best experience possible, provide a welcoming, engaging, and secure platform, and having a customer service support team that is not only dependable, and here when you need them, but to also make ourselves available to you 24/7!
